# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What browsers are supported?

SecureGrid Monitor supports the latest versions of Chrome, Microsoft Edge, and Firefox.

---

## How often are alerts updated?

Alerts update in real time. The system refreshes automatically every two minutes.

---

## Who can access SecureGrid Monitor?

Access is restricted to authorized internal users with approved credentials.

---

## Can reports be shared externally?

Exported reports may only be shared with authorized stakeholders in accordance with internal data policies.

---

## What should I do if the system is slow?

1. Verify network connectivity.
2. Refresh your browser.
3. Confirm there are no active system maintenance notifications.

---

## Where can I find system updates or maintenance notices?

System updates and maintenance notifications appear on the Dashboard banner and are distributed via email when applicable.
